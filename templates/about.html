About

This is a project that reports the cost of living in the United States by state and year.

The Cost of Living Index (COLI) is a measurement of the basic expenses of a location. It is also known as the Consumer Price Index. The cost of living of an average city in the country is set to 100.0 and every other location is assessed based on that scale. For example, in 2023, New York State has a COLI of 148.2, meaning it is 48.2% more expensive to live in New York than in an average state.
